# Audit Report: Fraud and Compliance Findings

**Report Date:** 2026-07-06

Executive Summary

This audit report details findings from an analysis of expense data. A total of 23 findings were identified, comprising 8 high-severity and 15 medium-severity issues. High-severity findings include instances of expense structuring, where multiple smaller expenses were submitted by an individual within a short timeframe, and duplicate expense claims. Medium-severity findings highlight significant spikes in departmental spending categories that exceeded predefined thresholds and individual expenses that were statistical outliers.

---
